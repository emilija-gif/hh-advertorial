About Best Patio Products

We're a small team of engineers, contractors, and journalists who believe homeowners deserve better information about the products they install in their backyards.

Best Patio Products was founded in 2015 with a simple premise: the outdoor living industry was full of marketing claims and short on verifiable data. Homeowners were spending $5,000 to $70,000 on pergolas, patio covers, and gazebos based on glossy photos and vague promises like "built strong" and "premium quality."

We thought that was unacceptable. So we started testing.

What We Do Differently

Every product we review goes through a rigorous evaluation process. We don't just unbox it and share first impressions. We read the engineering reports (when manufacturers provide them). We measure wall thicknesses with calipers. We check aluminum grades against the Aluminum Design Manual. We verify wind and snow load claims against ASCE7 and IBC standards.

When a manufacturer won't share their engineering data, we say so. When their marketing claims don't match the specs, we say that too.

How We Make Money

Transparency matters. Here's how we keep the lights on:

Affiliate commissions. When you click a link in one of our reviews and make a purchase, we may earn a commission from the manufacturer. This never affects our ratings, rankings, or editorial conclusions. We've given negative reviews to products from our highest-paying affiliate partners.

Display advertising. Standard ad placements on our site generate a portion of our revenue. We limit ad density to preserve the reading experience.

What we don't do: We don't accept payment for positive reviews. We don't let brands approve or edit our content before publication. We don't suppress negative findings. If a manufacturer refuses to provide engineering data for verification, that itself becomes part of the review.
